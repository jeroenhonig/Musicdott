- **Growth Analytics**: Track enrollment trends, teacher performance, and identify opportunities for expansion
- **Automated Billing**: Subscription management that scales automatically with student count
- **Multi-Location Support**: Manage multiple locations and teaching rooms with unified scheduling and reporting

- **Interactive Lessons**: Create multimedia lessons with sheet music, audio files, videos, and practice exercises

- **Progress Visualization**: See improvement over time with clear charts and milestone celebrations
